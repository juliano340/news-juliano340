## Resumo em 3 bullets
- **Topico 1**: Model choice depends on task complexity, not platform popularity.
- **Topico 2**: Claude excels at structured reasoning and programming tasks.
- **Topico 3**: Gemini offers Deep Research for multi-source searches; ChatGPT is a versatile generalist.

## Por que isso importa para devs
Selecting the right AI model directly impacts development efficiency, cost control, and output quality. Matching model capabilities to task complexity prevents over-engineering or under-performance, while understanding usage limits avoids unexpected throttling or extra costs.

## O que muda na pratica
- Map each project’s complexity and data needs before picking a model.
- Test lightweight models (e.g., Gemini 3 Flash, Claude Haiku) for quick queries to reduce costs.
- Reserve advanced models (e.g., GPT-5.2 Pro, Claude Opus) for complex analysis or code generation.

## Contexto rapido
